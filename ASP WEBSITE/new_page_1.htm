HISTORY OF BOTANY DEPARTMENT

                             

                    Under Graduate  Programme in Science was started in 1962 with three subjects; Botany , Chemistry and Zoology. The department of Botany  flourished under the dynamic leadership of 

Dr. Shanti Devi Vimal and the number of faculty members was raised  to six in  1971.

              

                 With the growing need of girls education in this region and  to keep pace with  needs of society , M.Sc. Programme was started in the year 2000  under the energetic leadership and the coordinator ship of the  Reader and Incharge of Department Dr. (Mrs.) Kusum Agarwal .

 

        In this very short period of time, the hard work and dedication of all faculty members, has provided a very sound footing to the department. M.Sc. students are not only  doing extremely well in their course curriculum but also they are  performing excellent in delivering informative seminars through presentation on very recent topics, writing assignments related to course of their  study and attending class room quizes. Guest lectures on several important topics by guest faculty of repute is an important component of the curriculum.

  

            The members of the department have always been on the forefront of learning and are regularly attending Conferences, Workshops, Symposia and Refresher Courses. Organization of excursions to the places of scientific importance as well as plant collection tours including the organization of Workshop and training programmes to elevate the technical know-how of the students   and faculty  have been the healthy practices of the department.
