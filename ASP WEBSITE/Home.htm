About Us

 

Every business should be completely concerned with

beauty - it is after all a collective human need.

 If :

 "HUMAN NATURE IS TO LIVE IN THE PAST",

 then :

 "TO CHANGE THE WORLD IS, TO CHANGE HUMAN NATURE".

The word "Chirakshii" means 'Colorful Eyes', which motivates to drive our imaginary forces towards making this world more colorful and beautiful, with the combination of factors, such as

1. DESIGNS based on human experience, social behaviors, global, economic and political issues, physical and mental interaction, form, vision, and a rigorous understanding and desire for contemporary culture.

2. MANUFACTURING based on another collective group of criteria: capital investment, market share, production ease, dissemination, growth, distribution, maintenance, service, performance, quality, ecological issues and sustainability.

Therefore, our CEO, Ms. Akshii Jain, wants to combine these factors to shape our objects, inform our forms, our physical space, visual culture and our contemporary human experience. These quantitative constructs shape business, identity, brand and value.

ChitrAkshii Interiors is committed to design excellence, responsibility and sustainability. Dedicated to the creation of custom interiors, the company prides itself on combining architecture and interior design to find unique and aesthetically pleasing solutions.

ChitrAkshii Interiors completes projects tailored to the individual styles and needs of its clients, on budget and on time.

ChitrAkshii Interiors is known for creating dynamic spaces that enrich the personal and business and lives of their clients and staff.

This is the business of beauty.
